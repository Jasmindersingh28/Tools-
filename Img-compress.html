Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges, watermarks, or registration required.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can keep the original format or convert to another format during compression. WebP typically offers the best compression ratios.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What's the maximum image size I can compress?

You can compress images up to 20MB in size. For best results with very large images, we recommend resizing them first before compression.
